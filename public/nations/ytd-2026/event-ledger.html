Coverage: 2026-01-01 through 2026-07-15 Records: 42 Status date: July 15, 2026

A deliberately selected ledger of developments judged material to Indigenous economic, business, finance, ownership, rights, policy, and major-project matters in Canada. Inclusion is not a claim of exhaustiveness. Amounts are Canadian dollars unless stated otherwise.

Reading the ledger

Stage is the controlling field. A proposal, option, consultation, or financing commitment is not treated as a closed transaction. Confidence grades the factual record available for the event, not the likelihood that the underlying project will succeed.
