## Feature Ideas (Researched, Not Yet Built)

### Tier 1 — Carry Cards
Digital wallet-style reference cards for use in the moment. NIHB coverage card (at the dentist), Section 87 quick reference (at CRA), rights summary (dealing with institutions), emergency numbers (one-tap calling). Each card: front (the answer) and back (the details). The feature that keeps the app installed permanently.

### Tier 2 — Seasonal Wheel UI
App shifts with Indigenous seasonal calendars (thirteen-moon or regional variants). Content priorities rotate. Palette warms and cools. Home greeting changes. Makes the app feel alive and culturally grounded.

### Tier 3 — Ceremony and Gathering Planner
Potlatch, feast, giveaway, memorial — each has real costs. Tool treats ceremony prep as a legitimate financial goal with its own categories. "An app that treats potlatch preparation on the same level as saving for a home."

### Tier 4 — Interactive Stories
Financial education as text-message-style conversations. Follow a character navigating their first job, Section 87, NIHB. 3-5 minutes, choose-your-path. More engaging than articles.

### Tier 5 — Community Features
- Housing Navigator (on-reserve housing programs, CMHC, MLG, renovation funding)
- NIHB Medical Travel Claim Helper
- Traditional Food Economy Calculator
- Know Your Rights Guide (situation-based: "I'm leaving a relationship on reserve")
- Elder Benefits Maximizer + Estate Planning
- Youth "First Everything" Guide
- Scam Alert Network
- Document Vault (encrypted on-device storage for Status card, band docs)
- Community Resource Map (crowdsourced services directory)

## Key Research Findings
- Tools over content, 3:1 ratio (NZ Sorted research) — calculators as primary, articles as support
- Life-event navigation beats topic navigation (My Money Dream, Australia)
- "Only goes up" progress metrics — never punish absence (Headspace pattern)
- Easy re-engagement after absence — "Welcome back" + 60-second recap (Duolingo)
- Stealth education through utility — teach through tools, not lessons (Cash App)
- No app like this exists globally for Indigenous communities — first of kind
